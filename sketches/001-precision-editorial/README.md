# Precision Editorial

Homepage concept 001 for Justin Chong's portfolio. Open `index.html` directly in a browser; there are no build steps or external assets.

## Design stance

Precision Editorial is the recruiter-first direction: warm paper, near-black ink, a disciplined 12-column grid, and selective rust-red emphasis. It aims to feel exacting rather than austere. Dense evidence, compact annotations, and intentionally unequal project treatments replace generic cards and decorative technology imagery.

## Key choices

- The opening viewport pairs the approved positioning with visible résumé and selected-work actions, an explicit practice range, and defensible proof immediately below.
- Metalynx is the visual flagship. Its generated SVG model space demonstrates the analysis concept without presenting invented UI as historical product evidence. A two-state cohort interaction connects spatial analysis to sample inspection.
- Kikan uses a sequential, keyboard-operable stage inspector to communicate orchestration and verification. Its current evidence is explicitly framed as early-stage dogfooding, not proven production autonomy.
- Aival is deliberately restrained and high-level. A typographic leadership statement and newly drawn conceptual delivery flow avoid exposing proprietary architecture or sensitive deployment detail.
- Engineering depth uses editorial stories rather than another project grid. Netcraft's verified 13× and 7× memory improvements carry the quantified proof.
- AI Lab entries look like working notes and are clearly labeled as experiments. Operating principles are tied back to project evidence rather than left as soft-skill claims.
- System fonts, inline CSS/JS, semantic landmarks, a skip link, visible focus, 44px controls, reduced-motion handling, and mobile-specific visual recomposition keep the concept dependency-free and accessible.

## Trade-offs

- The Metalynx visual is art-directed and representative, not a data-accurate product demo. It is labeled accordingly.
- Routes such as `/resume`, `/contact`, and case-study paths are structural concept links and require their corresponding pages in the final site.
- The page is intentionally information-dense. The asymmetric hierarchy, section rules, and alternating media treatments are used to preserve scanning speed rather than reducing content.
- Interactions progressively enhance already-present descriptions; the core portfolio narrative remains readable if JavaScript is unavailable.

## Best use

Use this direction when the primary objective is fast hiring-manager comprehension with credible paths into technical depth. It is the strongest fit for Technical Lead, Staff/Principal, platform/data, and AI-product engineering applications where builder and leader evidence must be visible in the same scan.
