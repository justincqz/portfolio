01 / PRODUCT + VISUAL COMPUTING

Metalynx
Scatter

An interactive way for AI developers to interrogate learned representations, find overlap and outliers, and move from a spatial pattern to the samples behind it.

Designed and implemented as a complete product: numerical processing, OpenGL visualization, filtering, interaction design, and export.

Read the full case study

Analysis path

  1. Import model outputsLOGITS + METADATA / CSV
  2. Reduce the representationPCA / THREE DIMENSIONS
  3. Map structure and separationOPENGL POINT CLOUD

  5. Curate samples for analysisGALLERY + CSV EXPORT

Tested with and refined through feedback from individual AI researchers, including researchers affiliated with NASA, Waymo, and Mastercard. These were individual researchers, not organizational customers, partners, adopters, or endorsements.
